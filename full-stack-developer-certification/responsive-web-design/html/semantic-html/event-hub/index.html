Macbeth

Come one and all, ye goodly gentle folk, and cast your eye upon a treacherous tale of witches, wanting, heavy tyrant's yoke. Cast cruelly on the land of Scotland fair, and in advance of wicked hallow's eve, we conjured up a merry gentle band of tall tale-tellers for a day's reprieve in hopes that they could lend us both a hand. And so, good folk, please catch with bated breath, a most shambolic telling of Macbeth! Featuring yon most noble stooges: Red as Lord Macbeth - Thane of Glamis and Blue as Lord Macduff - Thane of Fife, and ye honored and illustrious comedians, to whome the breaking of our brightly-colored stars is their singular purpose.

Date: October 28, 2022
