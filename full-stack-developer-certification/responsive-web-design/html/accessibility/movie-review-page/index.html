Movie Review: The Passion of the Christ

Directed by Mel Gibson and realeased on Ash Wednesday 2004, "The Passion of the Christ" depicts the final twelve hoursof Jesus' life. It begins with the Agony in the Garden of Gethsemane, continues with the betrayal of Judas Iscariot to the Pharisees, the trial before the Sanhedrin and then Pontius Pilate, the Flagellation of Christ, the suffering of Mary, as prophesied by Simeon, the crucifixion and death of Jesus, and ends with a brief depiction of the resurrection. The narrative is interspersed with moments in Jesus' life, such as the Last Supper, the Sermon on the Mount, and moments of Jesus' early life.
